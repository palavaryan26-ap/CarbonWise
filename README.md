# 🌱 CarbonWise

### Track. Reduce. Sustain.

**Making sustainability measurable, actionable, and accessible for everyone.**

CarbonWise is an AI-powered web application designed to help individuals understand, track, and reduce their carbon footprint through personalized insights, gamified sustainability challenges, and data-driven recommendations.

---

## 📖 Problem Statement

Many individuals want to adopt an environmentally conscious lifestyle but lack awareness of how their daily activities contribute to global carbon emissions.

Existing solutions are often overly complex, difficult to interpret, and fail to provide actionable guidance.

### Our Solution

CarbonWise simplifies carbon footprint management by:
- Calculating emissions from daily activities
- Visualizing environmental impact through analytics
- Providing personalized AI-powered recommendations
- Encouraging sustainable habits through challenges and rewards

---

## 🚀 Core Features

### 📊 Carbon Footprint Calculator
- Transportation
- Electricity Consumption
- Food Habits and Diet Choices
- Waste Generation and Recycling

### 📈 Analytics Dashboard
- Real-time Carbon Footprint Overview
- Emission Breakdown
- Trend Analysis
- Sustainability Score

### 🤖 AI-Powered Recommendations
- Personalized Suggestions
- Estimated CO₂ Reduction Impact
- Difficulty Ratings
- Actionable Daily Improvement Plans

### 🏆 Progress Tracking & Eco Challenges
- Daily Tracking
- Weekly Reports
- Monthly Reports
- Historical Trends
